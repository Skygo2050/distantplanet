~About Us~

"Distant Planets" is a project made by third year students of "E&TC" from "Thakur College of Engineering & Technology".

Distant Planets is a PBL(Project Base Learning) project which is to make an Educational game on subject Physics.

(Standard - 11th and 12th/junior college 1st and 2nd year)

Vision:

Make Educational game more interesting. And provide the students a efficient way of online education. So they can study while enjoying game.

Mission:

Test the knowledge of Player on the topic and expand their knowledge on topics of the subject.
